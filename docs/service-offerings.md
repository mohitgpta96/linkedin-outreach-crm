## Service Tiers

### Tier 1: Fractional Project Manager (Ongoing)
**What:** Embedded part-time PM for your team — attend standups,
manage sprints, handle stakeholder communication, keep delivery
on track.

**Best For:**
- Startups scaling from 5 to 20+ engineers
- Teams that need PM structure but can't justify a full-time hire
- Companies between PM hires (gap coverage)

**Deliverables:**
- Sprint planning and backlog grooming
- Daily/weekly standup facilitation
- Stakeholder status reports (weekly)
- Risk register and mitigation tracking
- Retrospectives and process improvements
- Tool setup and workflow optimization (Jira/Asana/Notion)

**Time Commitment:** 10-20 hours/week
**Rate Range:** $30-50/hr (depending on scope and duration)
**Minimum Engagement:** 4 weeks

---

### Tier 2: Project Setup & Structure (One-Time)
**What:** Set up the entire project management infrastructure
from scratch — tools, workflows, templates, documentation.

**Best For:**
- Teams starting a new product/project with no PM process
- Companies switching PM tools (e.g., Trello → Jira)
- Founders who need structure but will self-manage after setup

**Deliverables:**
- Tool configuration (Jira/Asana/Notion) with custom workflows
- Sprint/iteration structure design
- Template pack: PRDs, sprint reports, risk registers, RACI matrix
- Team onboarding documentation
- 1-week handover support after setup

**Pricing:** $500-1500 (fixed price, scope-dependent)
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

---

### Tier 3: Project Recovery / Audit
**What:** Diagnose why a project is off-track and create a
concrete recovery plan.

**Best For:**
- Projects that have missed multiple deadlines
- Teams where scope creep has killed momentum
- Stakeholders who've lost visibility into progress

**Deliverables:**
- Current-state assessment (interviews + tool/data review)
- Root cause analysis document
- Recovery roadmap with prioritized actions
- Revised timeline and resource plan
- Optional: Stay on as fractional PM to execute recovery

**Pricing:** $300-800 for audit only; ongoing PM at Tier 1 rates
**Timeline:** 3-5 days for audit

---

**What:** Set up AI-powered project management workflows using
Claude Code, including custom automation, documentation generation,
and reporting pipelines.

**Best For:**
- Tech-forward teams wanting to leverage AI in their PM process

**Deliverables:**

- Automated report generation pipelines

- Team training on using AI-assisted PM workflows

**Pricing:** $800-2000 (fixed price, scope-dependent)
**Timeline:** 1-2 weeks

## Messaging Rules for Service Mentions

### In Connection Requests
- NEVER mention services, rates, or deliverables
- Focus entirely on THEM, not what you sell

### In Day 3 Follow-Up
- Share a resource related to your expertise (not a pitch)
- Demonstrate capability through giving, not telling

### In Day 7 Follow-Up
- Can offer a specific, bounded free deliverable (audit, template)
- Frame as "something I built that might help" — not a sales pitch

### In Value Offer (After Engagement)
- NOW you can mention what you do — but keep it solution-focused
- Lead with their pain point, not your service description
- Use specific past results: "Helped a Series B team cut sprint
  planning from 4 hours to 90 minutes"
- Always give an easy, low-commitment next step
